About Us.

Girls are a powerful force for change in Africa, but they are considered as weak individuals hence, they are oppressed, humiliated and are not given equal opportunities in the society , education and employment.

Heroes GIrls is a girl empowerment initiative that aims at solving several challenges faced by young girls by providing education, advices and sharing experiences to overcome early pregnancies and marriages. through articles, books and videos.
